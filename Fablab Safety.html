Fablab Safety

General safety rules and regulations

Students are required to wear collared shirt or t-shirt with full-length trousers. Students are not allowed to wear loose-sleeved clothing and jewellery. Also, students should mot use compressed air to clean their clothing. Covered shoes are to be worn at all time without open tops or open toes. For students with long hair, they are required to tie their hair up to avoid entanglement with rotating machinery. Eating and drinking are not allows in laboratories and workshops. Phones are to switch off while working in laboratories and workshops.
